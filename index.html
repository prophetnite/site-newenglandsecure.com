Our Story

Newengland Secure services was started out of the need for better security. Our core staff worked in MSP services where we all saw many things lacking, lost passwords, poor documentation, and no way to know if anything or anyone was compromised.

With that we started a cloud based security company. 90% of our services are based in the cloud, SECURITY FIRST and fully audited. User reliability and ease of use are #1 for us. Your IT should work for you, not against you.
